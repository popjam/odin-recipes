Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish made with a yeasted flatbread crust and topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It's a versatile and delicious meal that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C). If you are using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or parchment paper dusted with cornmeal.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Top with mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, olives, mushrooms, bell peppers, and onions.
  6. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, oregano, and basil.
  7. Transfer the pizza to the preheated pizza stone or a baking sheet.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before slicing and serving.
